Current Build · 2025–2026

PROJECT-001  ·  ACTIVE
Novel SOS1 Inhibitors — Shutting Down the KRAS On-Switch

KRAS is one of the most common cancer mutations on Earth — and one of the hardest to drug. The trick is upstream: SOS1 is the protein that flips KRAS on by swapping its GDP for GTP. I'm designing small molecules that wedge into the SOS1–KRAS interface and block that exchange entirely. No exchange, no activation, no tumor signal.

The Inventor's Toolkit
